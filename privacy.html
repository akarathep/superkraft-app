Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Overview

Superkraft AR Tracker ("the App") is an internal business application developed and operated by Superkraft Co., Ltd. for internal use only. This application connects to QuickBooks Online to retrieve invoice and financial data for internal business management purposes.

Data We Access

The App accesses the following data from QuickBooks Online: invoice records, customer names, invoice amounts, payment status, and due dates. This data is used solely for internal accounts receivable management.

How We Use Your Data

All data accessed through QuickBooks Online is used exclusively for internal business operations of Superkraft Co., Ltd. Data is stored securely in Google Firebase and is accessible only to authorised Superkraft team members.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. All data remains within Superkraft Co., Ltd. internal systems.

Data Security

All data is protected by Firebase Authentication requiring email and password login. Access is restricted to authorised personnel only.

Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy please contact Superkraft Co., Ltd.
